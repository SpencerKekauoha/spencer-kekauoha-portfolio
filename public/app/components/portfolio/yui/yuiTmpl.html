YUI

In Okinawan, Yui means to connect, collobrate, or come together as a group to acommplish a task. Yui was created to connect designers with businesses to build projects. CSS animations were used to make the logo stand-out.

Featured Designer

Yui features a unique designer weekly to help them gain exposure. Elements of parallax and jQuery were used in the design.

Image Flow

Using the Mean Stack, images are stored in a database after a user makes a post. Additionally, they can update or delete their post as well.

Social Media & Exposure

Utilizing PassportJS, users can sign in and post their designs. JavaScript logic is used to allow users that are signed in to like other posts.
